Our Mission

We are proud that J&T products are a part of or consumer's everyday life. We always strive to provide high quality plastic products that will still be competitively priced for years to come. We make sure to continuously improve and innovate our products to consistently satisfy the needs of our consumers. We would also like to continue to develop our brand to become the number one plastic products brand in the country. We also make it a point to be socially responsible to the stakeholders of the company.
